7-Day Return & Exchange

We want you to love your purchase! If you're not completely satisfied, you can return or exchange your product within 7 days of delivery.

Conditions for Returns/Exchange

  • Product must be unused and in original packaging
  • All tags and labels should be intact
  • Product should not be washed or altered
  • Original invoice/receipt required
  • Product should be in the same condition as received

Non-Returnable Items

  • Products that have been used or washed
  • Items without original packaging or tags
  • Products purchased during clearance sales (unless defective)
  • Items damaged due to misuse or negligence

How to Return/Exchange

  1. WhatsApp us at 098872 59471 within 7 days of receiving your order
  2. Send clear photos of the product and its packaging
  3. Mention reason for return/exchange
  4. We'll arrange pickup (in Jaipur) or provide return shipping address
  5. Once we receive and inspect, we'll process your request

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned product:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Amount will be credited to your original payment method
  • For Cash on Delivery orders, refund via bank transfer
  • Exchanges will be shipped immediately after inspection

Exchange Options

You can exchange for:

  • Same product in different color
  • Same product in different size (for kurtis/suits)
  • Different product of equal or higher value

Defective/Damaged Products

  • We'll provide immediate replacement at no extra cost
